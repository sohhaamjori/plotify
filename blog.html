Top 5 Tips Before Buying a Plot

Buying land is a huge investment. Here are 5 tips to consider before finalizing your decision:

  • ✔️ Verify land title and legal documents
  • ✔️ Ensure clear demarcation and land measurements
  • ✔️ Check zoning laws and land use permissions
  • ✔️ Inspect road access, water, and electricity availability
  • ✔️ Evaluate the area’s development potential & resale value
