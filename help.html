Help Center

1. How to List a Plot?

To list a plot, go to the "List Your Plot" section, fill in all the required details including location, price, and description, and submit. You’ll receive a confirmation email once it's live.

2. How to Edit or Delete a Listed Plot?

Login to your account, go to your Dashboard, find your listed plot and choose either "Edit" or "Delete" option. Changes take effect immediately.

3. How to Subscribe to a Plan?

Visit the "Subscription" page, choose your desired plan, and make payment using available options. You'll get additional benefits like featured listings and more visibility.

4. I Didn't Receive Email Confirmation?

Please check your spam or junk folder. If still not received, contact us at support@plotify.in and we’ll assist you quickly.

5. Can I Contact the Seller Directly?

Yes, once you find a plot you're interested in, you can use the contact form or provided phone/email to directly reach out to the seller.
